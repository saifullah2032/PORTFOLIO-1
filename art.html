Hi, It's Rayan

Hi, I'm Rayan, an artist passionate about exploring a range of styles, from detailed pen and pencil drawings to captivating glass art and digital creations. Art is more than a hobby for me—it's a way to express creativity and explore new techniques. Each medium I work with offers a unique opportunity to convey different emotions and ideas, making every project a new adventure.
In my professional life, I'm a web and Android developer, as well as a UI/UX designer. This blend of art and technology allows me to approach both fields with a unique perspective, constantly seeking innovative solutions and designs. I also offer customization services to ensure each piece is tailored to my client's vision, blending personal preferences with artistic flair. I find joy in merging my artistic and technical skills to create something truly special and meaningful.
